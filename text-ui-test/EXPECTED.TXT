Caprese Salad
Ingredients needed: 
1. Tomatoes
2. Mozzarella
3. Basil
4. Olive Oil 
Method: 
1. Cut tomatoes into 1 inch slices
2. Cut mozzarella into similar slices
3. Arrange slices on plate
4. Drizzle with olive oil
